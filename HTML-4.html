Top 3 Things I Need for Playing my Keyboard

  • Keyboard
  • Electricity
  • Good Mood

Steps to Get Started

  1. Step 1: Be in the mood to play the keyboard (because when I am not in the mood, I am always off-key. It is hard to keep up with the rhythm since I get distracted by other things).
  2. Step 2: Get the keyboard.
  3. Step 3: Pick a song to play.
  4. Step 4: Search for "(Insert Song) Piano Tutorial."
  5. Step 5: Practice for at least 2 hours.
  6. Step 6: Repeat.
